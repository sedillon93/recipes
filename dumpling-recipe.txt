Ingredients:
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup milk

Directions:
1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in medium size bowl.

2. Cut in butter until crumbly.

3. Stir in milk to make a soft dough.

4. Drop by spoonfuls into boiling stew.

5. Cover and simmer 15 minutes without lifting lid.

6. Serve.
